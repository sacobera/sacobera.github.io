Project: Lo-Cal

Lo-Cal Homepage

Context: A DevMountain project with the goal of creating a simple app that has simple functions.

Description: A website with a loan and mortgage calculator. Created with HTML, CSS, Javascript and API.

Loan Calculator




This calculator takes a user's loan amount, interest rate, and months to pay to determine how much their monthly pay would be.

Mortgage Calculator


The mortgage calculator is created with API from mlcalc.com that allows the user to play around with different factors that goes into paying for a mortgage to estimate their monthly pay rates and what they can afford.
